Classes & Workshops

As a teacher, I offer art classes, creative workshops, and one-on-one creative guidance.

My approach is rooted in exploration, self-expression, and the belief that creativity is a vital part of being human. I enjoy creating spaces where people can experiment, reflect, play, and reconnect with their own creative voice.

I do not teach on a regular schedule but I am always open to offering classes or workshops when the right opportunity, group, or setting comes together.

Possible offerings may include:

  • Small group art classes
  • One-on-one creative sessions
  • Community workshops
  • School, library, or organization-based classes
  • Custom creative experiences
